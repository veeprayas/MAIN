App Information

The app mainly aims at predicting the amount of urea or SOP needed by specifying the amount of NPK content present in the soil

There is a second function where the farmar can upload the picture of the leaf of a particular and using image processing techniques, we would determine whether the crop is diseased or not. this functionality can be compared with the help of a table provided by the class and the disease relating to it as the output appears on the screen as a probability matrix.

The page has a sign in and a sign up page where one could register and login.
